FAQ

Questions, answered.

Open only what matters to you.

Do I need technical skills to set this up?
No. Vanguard handles the setup, logic and integrations. You provide your services, hours, staff details and customer process.
Can it handle WhatsApp conversations on its own?
Yes. The AI receptionist can answer common questions, collect details, guide customers toward bookings and hand over when human help is needed.
How do payments work?
Vanguard currently uses secure EFT payments. After your proposal is approved, you receive an official invoice with banking details.
Can I use my own branding?
Yes. Customer-facing flows can match your business name, tone, services and communication style depending on the package.
Is my data separated from other businesses?
Yes. Each client system is built with separated records, workflows and access so information is not mixed between businesses.
Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly support is flexible, but setup fees cover the custom build. Final cancellation terms are confirmed in your proposal.
